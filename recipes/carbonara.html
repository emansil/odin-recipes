Carbonara

This recipe for grilled Carbonara doesn't call for fancy marinades or mercurial methods. All that's needed is quality ingredients and a hot grill.

The main question is, which carbonara is best?

I prefer wild-caught carbonara because it has more flavor, but if you're dealing with eaters who are mild fish fans, a quality farm-raised carbonara will have a more mellow flavor.
